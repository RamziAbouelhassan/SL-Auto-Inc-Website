How the appointment flow works

A simple repair process built around communication and practical next steps.

1

Call and describe the issue

Tell us what the vehicle is doing and whether there are warning lights, noises, or drivability symptoms.

2

Inspection and diagnosis

We inspect the vehicle, identify the likely cause, and explain what repairs or maintenance are recommended.

3

Approval before work proceeds

We review the next steps and proceed with the agreed service so you know what is being done.

4

Pickup and follow-up recommendations

We let you know what was completed and flag any maintenance items to keep an eye on next.

Before you call

  • Vehicle year, make, and model
  • Dashboard warning lights (if any)
  • When the issue happens (cold start, highway, braking, turning)
  • Recent repairs or maintenance
  • Best callback number
