Our Philosophy

Built on Trust and Precision

We bridge the gap between expensive dealership repairs and untrustworthy corner shops.

Why do drivers choose SL Auto Inc?

We started this shop because we saw a recurring problem in the auto repair industry: miscommunication between mechanics and customers.

Too often, drivers pay for repairs they didn't need, or worse, they pay for a "guess" that doesn't fix their original issue. At SL Auto Inc, we replace components based on hard data from factory-level scan tools and meticulous testing, never on a hunch.

The Right Fix, First Time

We don't throw parts at problems. Our rigorous diagnostic tree isolates faults effectively, saving you money by avoiding unnecessary parts.

Dealership Alternative

Maintain your new car warranty with our factory-scheduled maintenance, while saving up to 30% compared to dealership overhead pricing.

Plain English Explanations

We avoid confusing mechanical jargon. We'll show you the exact part that failed, explain what it does, and why it needs replacement.
