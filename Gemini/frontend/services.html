Our Expertise

Comprehensive Automotive Care

We focus on specialized diagnostics and proactive maintenance to maximize vehicle lifespan.

🔍

Check Engine Diagnostics

We use factory-level scan tools to communicate directly with your vehicle's computers. We don't just clear codes; we find the root cause of electrical faults, emission failures, and completely resolve drivability issues.

🛑

Brake Systems

Squeaking, grinding, or soft pedal? We replace pads, machine or replace rotors, flush brake fluid, and service calipers to restore factory stopping power with premium ceramic or OEM parts.

🚙

Steering & Suspension

Is your ride bouncy or making clunking noises over bumps? We replace worn struts, shocks, control arms, tie rods, and bushings to keep your vehicle hugging the road tightly and tracking perfectly straight.

⚡

Battery & Charging

Slow cranks or dead mornings usually point to the battery, alternator, or starter. We load-test the entire charging system to guarantee you'll never be stranded in extreme Calgary weather.

🛢️

Fluid Maintenance

Engine oil, transmission fluid, coolant, power steering, and diff fluids degrade over time. We perform complete fluid exchanges according to your manufacturer's specific mileage intervals.

📋

Pre-Purchase Inspections

Buying a used car? Bring it to us first. We perform a rigorous 100+ point bumper-to-bumper inspection to uncover hidden accident damage, leaks, or impending major repairs.

Don't see your specific concern listed?

We handle almost all mechanical repairs on modern import and domestic vehicles.
