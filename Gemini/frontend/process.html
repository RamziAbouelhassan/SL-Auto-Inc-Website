How We Work

Our Transparent Process

We believe in zero surprises. You are completely in control of what repairs are authorized on your vehicle.

01

Intake & Consultation

It starts with booking online or calling us. When you drop off the vehicle, we'll listen carefully to the sounds you're hearing, the driveability issues you're experiencing, and note down all your concerns upfront.

02

Thorough Inspection

Our technicians take your car into the bay and perform a comprehensive visual and digital inspection. We hook up diagnostic scanners and perform test drives if necessary to accurately reproduce the issue.

03

The Estimate & Approval

We build a prioritized estimate. We'll call you to explain exactly what is broken (Safety/Critical), what is wearing out (Recommended soon), and what is totally fine. We do not turn a wrench or charge you a cent until you explicitly approve the work.

04

Execution & Quality Control

Upon your approval, we order premium parts and perform the repair efficiently. After completion, we test-drive the vehicle to verify that the original concern has been fully resolved.

05

Pickup & Review

When you arrive to pick up your keys, we will go over the invoice with you, show you the old broken parts if requested, and answer any lingering questions about the repair.
